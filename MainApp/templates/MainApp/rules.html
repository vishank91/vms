Guidelines for Parents

Do's

1. Use the school diary as a means of communication with the class teacher and cheek the diary tour Home Work and the teacher's remarks.if any

2. Sign the test copies/papers as and when sent Home.

3. See that your ward has all the text books,items of stationary,craft materials etc. right in the beginning of the session

4. Ensure that all the books and note books are covered with brown paper and labeled properly.

5. Ensure that your ward comes to school in proper uniform with polished shoes, trimmed hair and clean nails.

6. Send the children regularly and punctually to school. No absence will be permitted without a leave application and late comers will note be allowed to sit in the class.

7. Both the parents must attend the Parent's-Teacher's Meet.

8. Pay the fees as per schedule.

9. Intimate the school in case of change in address, telephone number etc.

10. Help in the developmental work of the school whenever necessary.

Don't

1. Do not send the child to school in case of an infectious disease, and a medical certificate should be furnished from a medical authority at tch time ofrejoining the school after illness.

2. Do not walk into the classroom or meet the teacher in the classroom. Please see the teacher with prior appointment through Principal .

3. Do not visit the teacher at their residence.

4. Do not send money or valuable articles with the students. The school takes no responsibility in case those are lost.

5. Students shall not be allowed to leave the school campus before the school is over.

Guidelines for Students

1. All the students must posses the school diary, text-books, note books and stationery etc.

2. Students are ads iced not to bring valuable articles to the school. The school will not responsible for any such loss.

3. Application for leave must be submitted a day in advance. It should be counter-singed by the parent/guardian.

4. Students must come in proper school uniform.

5. They must be clean and tidy.

6. Students should obey house captains and other students on duty.

7. Students are not allowed to leave the school during working hours unless the parents / guardian., whose signatures are on the admission forms and the School Diary, come in person.

8. School identity cards must be with the students in all working days.

9. Students should be regular in attendance.

10. Any damage to the school property by the student will have to be borne by the student's parents/guardian.

11. The school reserves the right to take a stern action even to strike off the name of Students from the School ills who are found guilty of misconduct_

12. Obey the rules oflibrary, laboratories.
